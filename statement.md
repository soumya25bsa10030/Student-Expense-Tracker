## **Problem Statement**
- Students struggle with financial management due to limited budgeting experience
- Manual expense tracking is time-consuming and often inaccurate  
- Lack of insights into spending patterns leads to poor financial decisions
- No personalized predictions for future expenses and budget planning
- Bill payments are frequently forgotten, resulting in late fees

## **Scope of the Project**
- **In Scope**: Expense tracking, budget management, spending analytics, bill reminders, prediction engine, data persistence
- **Out of Scope**: Bank integration, investment tracking, tax calculations, multi-user accounts, mobile app development

## **Target Users**
- **College/University Students** managing limited budgets
- **Young Adults** learning financial responsibility
- **International Students** handling expenses in new environments
- **Part-time Working Students** balancing income and expenses

## **High-level Features**
- Smart expense tracking with category classification
- Visual spending reports and analytics
- AI-powered expense prediction
- Automated bill payment reminders
- Budget setting and monitoring
- Secure data storage and backup
- User-friendly console interface
